Kourtney Kardashian Shares Photos from Her Gender Reveal Party

Publish date: 2024-09-29

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker are in their newlywed, expectant parent bliss.

The couple recently revealed they are expecting their first child together, and have since filled their social media feeds with happy photos of their journey, including their recent gender reveal party.

The Poosh founder posted various intimate photos on Instagram of her and the Blink-182 drummer at their baby bash. In them, she is seen sitting on his lap behind his drum set and playfully drumming.

She wears a body-hugging gray long-sleeve turtleneck dress with a black belt, over sheer tights and paired with diamond stud earrings. Barker wears his usual white tank, black pants, and black beanie.

"Little drummer boy coming soon," Kardashian wrote in the caption alongside a string of drum emojis.

Kardashian is already mom to Mason, 13; Penelope, 10; and Reign, 8. She shares them with ex Scott Disick. Barker, meanwhile, is dad to Landon, 19; Alabama, 17; and stepdaughter Atiana De La Hoya, 24. He shares them with ex-wife Shanna Moakler.

Kardashian and Barker voiced their wish to welcome children of their own early into their romance. The Lemme founder went through rounds of IVF to try to conceive before and after their wedding(s), and earlier this year became pregnant. She announced the happy news on June 17, in a video shared on Instagram of her jumping up and down at Barker's concert, holding a sign that read, "Travis I'm pregnant." It was a callback to Blink-182's music video for their hit "All The Small Things," where Barker's on-screen lover announces her pregnancy in the same way.
