Bride Rocks 8 Sophisticated Dresses for Wedding, Many Doubt Their Prices: &quot;Una Dey Pick Money?&quot;

Publish date: 2024-09-18
  • A bride caused uproar online after she displayed all her wedding outfits and what they were worth in a video
  • From her pre-wedding dresses to what she wore for her special day up till Thanksgiving, she slayed in all her dresses
  • She blended her attire with some expensive jewellery that made her the talk of many lips on social media

A bride, Tabitha Eddie, looked gorgeous as she dressed up in eight different wears for her classy wedding.

She proved she did not come to play as she turned up in a traditional look for her first outfit. Her green attire was combined with a matching headwrap, red beads on her neck, ears, and wrist, and perfect makeup. The outfit was worth N1 million.

The beautiful United Kingdom-based bride did not stop there as she slayed in another green dress worth N600k. Her next outfit, a short yellow dress, was a gift from the fashion designer (@concordia.atelier on Instagram).
