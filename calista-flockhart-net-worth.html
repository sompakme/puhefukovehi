Publish date: 2024-08-10
Calista Flockhart is an American actress born on November 11, 1964 in Freeport, IL. She is best known for her roles as Ally McBeal and Kitty Walker McCallister on Brothers & Sisters. For her performance in Ally McBeal, she won a Golden Globe Award, a Screen Actors Guild Award and a People's Choice Award. In 2015, she was cast in the CBS series Supergirl.

About

The star of Ally McBeal who also played Kitty Walker McCallister on Brothers & Sisters. For Ally McBeal, she received a Golden Globe Award, a Screen Actors Guild Award and a People's Choice Award. In 2015, she was cast in the CBS series Supergirl.

Before Fame

She worked as a waitress and an aerobics instructor while seeking auditions.

Trivia

She played the role of Helena in the 1999 film adaptation of A Midsummer Night's Dream alongside Christian Bale.

Family Life

She adopted a son named Liam in 2001 and she married legendary actor Harrison Ford in June 2010.
