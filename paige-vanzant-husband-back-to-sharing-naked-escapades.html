Paige VanZant &amp; Husband Back To Sharing Naked Escapades

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Paige VanZant and her husband have returned to sharing nude shots on VanZant's social media after taking a brief hiatus. The UFC fighter began posting the NSFW images on Instagram in April and had many fans divided, confused, and slightly disturbed.

The photo in question shows the Dancing With The Stars veteran and her husband, MMA fighter Austin Vanderford, with nothing but shoes on. The nude duo seemed to be on their way to walk their dog, and Paige strategically holds a “Rosé All Day” beach hat to cover her behind in the photo while Vanderford holds the leash. 

VanZant captioned the shot, “Tried the whole ‘clothing’ thing for awhile….. not for us.” 

Followers of the UFC fighter were split in their reactions to the photo, with users writing: 

“Oh no they’re back”

“The question is...who is taking the photo??”

“Attention seeking crap. You are better than this”

“The body is beautiful. Y'all are in love, and I love that. 🤟🤘🤙”

In a conversation with the Telegraph, Vanderford explained that he and his wife try to do something nude everyday, “whether we were working out, naked meal planning and cooking, gardening, out on the balcony, or just lounging about.” The pair have also been referred to as “The Van Nudists,” which Vanderford says is accurate.
