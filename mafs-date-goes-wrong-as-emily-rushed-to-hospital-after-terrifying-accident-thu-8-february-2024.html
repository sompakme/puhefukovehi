MAFS date goes wrong as Emily rushed to hospital after terrifying accident Thu 8 February 2024

Publish date: 2024-07-11

Emily from MAFS was involved in a terrifying accident during filming. The Married at First Sight Denver star was on a date with Brennon but was suddenly rushed to hospital after the bike she was on flipped.

The Married at First Sight cast member had a severe head injury after a bike incident. A worried Brennan rushed to his wife’s side during the honeymoon date, which involved riding quads in the forest.

Emily’s accident on MAFS

Emily and Brennan both smiled and yelled excitedly as they tear over the paths, until something threw Emily off course and suddenly flipped her bike after she drives the quad off the path.

When Brennan got to Emily after the crash, she’s bleeding from her head. The Married At First Sight crew takes charge as Brennan comforts Emily, who’s visibly shaken. She asked him, “Am I going to be okay?”

He responded, “You’re gonna be alright,” as he held her hand. Brennan is heard asking crews, “Can you call 911?” before Emily is then rushed to hospital as a result of the ATV accident.

Friend shuts down ‘fake’ rumors

When viewers claimed the blood “didn’t look real,” Emily’s friend hit back and said, “It was most definitely not staged! I’ve seen the pictures and get chills thinking she needs to relive this horrifying moment.”

It comes after fans wrote comments like, “I thought the same thing, that looked like lukewarm fake blood 🩸,” and, “I worked urgent care for over 15 years, Is it me or did that blood look hot pink? Hope she’s ok.”

Emily’s MAFS accident ‘predicted’ by co-star

Strangely, Austin told Emily he “hopes she wrecks the ATV” the day before the quad biking date. Emily also joked she could use some luck and Becca hoped that she would get lucky.

When fans discussed the idea of her head injury being staged, Austin kept being brought up. One viewer wrote, “Damn, Austin spoke Emily’s injury into existence. #marriedatfirstsight.”

“Omg not Austin saying he hopes they crash and Emily does crash and bust her head #mafs #MarriedAtFirstSight,” reacted a fellow Twitter user.
