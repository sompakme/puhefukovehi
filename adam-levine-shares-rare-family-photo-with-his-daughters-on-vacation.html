Adam Levine Shares Rare Family Photo With His Daughters on Vacation

Publish date: 2024-08-20

When you’re a rock star, you don’t take a chill family vacation. Adam Levine and his wife Behati Prinsloo went on the ultimate summer vacation to the “City of Love”, Paris, France — and they brought all three kids with them!

Related story Sources Reveal the One Reason Brad Pitt Sees His Kids, Which Hints at Their Tattered Relationship

The Maroon 5 frontman shared a rare family photo on Instagram with his wife and two eldest daughters yesterday. In the amazingly chic photo, one little girl is on Levine’s shoulders and the other is holding onto her mom’s hand as they walk up to the glass dome of the Louvre Museum.

“GANG,” the “Memories” singer captioned it.

Levine and Prinsloo are parents to Dusty Rose, 6, Gio Grace, 5, and a newborn baby who was born in Jan. 2023. They have not released the name or sex of the baby, although they have teased pictures of the little one before. Earlier this year, Levine was wrapped up in a cheating scandal, but he seems to be totally focused on his family now.

  • Behati Prinsloo Jokes Mom Life Is ‘Chicer in Paris’

    Prinsloo also shared photos of the family vacation on her Instagram Stories. The first one is a picture of her wearing matching gray sweatpants with her newborn baby while sitting on a hotel bed. “Jet lag hits different with three kids 🤪.”

    Another post showed Prinsloo and Levine each holding one of their girls as they walked around the Louvre admiring art. In one photo, her daughter holds up a baguette filled with ham and cheese, which Prinsloo jokes is “My love language.”

    Later, Prinsloo stands in front of the “Roue de Paris,” a huge Ferris wheel on the Place de la Concorde. She lifts her nursing scarf to reveal she is breastfeeding her baby in this elegant setting. “Everything feels chicer in Paris” she wrote, adding a tiny croissant emoji to cover her baby’s face.

  • Adam Levine Is Embracing ‘the Chaos’ at Home

    Levine gave an update about his family of five in a March 2023 interview with On-Air With Ryan Seacrest. 

    “It’s zoned defense, as they say. It’s awesome,” he said, per Billboard. “I love the chaos. I embrace the chaos.”

    That’s a good thing, because international travel with three kids is definitely chaotic!
