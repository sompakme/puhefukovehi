Early Life and Career

Frankie Edgar was born and raised in Toms River, New Jersey. He started wrestling at a young age and continued through college, which laid the foundation for his MMA career. Edgar made his professional MMA debut in 2005 and joined the UFC in 2007. His relentless work ethic and determination quickly made him a fan favorite.

Edgar’s UFC career is filled with notable victories and memorable fights. He won the UFC Lightweight Championship in 2010 and successfully defended it three times. His fights with Gray Maynard and BJ Penn are considered some of the best in UFC history.
