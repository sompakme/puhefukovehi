Who is Barstool Sports podcaster Kayce Smith?

Publish date: 2024-08-29

BARSTOOL Sports podcaster Kayce Smith has had a budding career since graduating from Texas A&M University in 2012.

She has firmly established herself as a versatile sports journalist over the years.

Who is Kayce Smith?

Born on October 25, 1988, in Texas, Kayce Smith is a reporter, sports broadcaster, podcaster, and multimedia personality.

She is well-known for her work on Barstool Sports and SiriusXM.

Previously, she worked for Fox Sports South where she was the sideline reporter for the NBA covering the Atlanta Hawks.

She also worked for NBC Sports Boston.

Prior to joining Barstool Sports, she studied at Texas A&M University.

After graduating in 2012, she worked for different outlets in various freelance positions.

She is known for her in-depth analysis of the NCAA circuit and interviews with players.
