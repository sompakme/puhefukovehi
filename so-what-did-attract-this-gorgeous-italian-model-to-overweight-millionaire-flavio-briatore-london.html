So what did attract this gorgeous Italian model to overweight millionaire Flavio Briatore? | London

Publish date: 2024-07-13

The 58-year-old lapped up the attention from his new wife, Italian Wonderbra model Elisabetta Gregoraci, during a romantic getaway in Sardinia yesterday.

The multi-millionaire, whose rather rotund physique clearly hasn't diminished his ability to attract women, hit the beach with the bikini-clad brunette before retiring to his luxury boat for some sun and affection.

Briatore married Gregoraci, who at 28 is 30 years his junior, in Rome last month at a 12th-century basilica near the Vatican.

Before the nuptials, Flavio was quoted as saying: 'I'm as excited as if a grand prix race was starting.'

The bride wore a succession of Roberto Cavalli-designed gowns throughout the day, including a strapless ivory sheath, complete with a seven-metre tulle veil.

F1 supremo Bernie Ecclestone was one of the more than 300 guests, along with Benetton group chairman Luciano Benetton and Italian prime minister Silvio Berlusconi.

Racing champion Fernando Alonso was put in charge of chauffeuring the newlyweds to their reception at the Castello di Torcrescenza, just north of Rome.
