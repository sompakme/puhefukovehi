See Jennifer Lopez Pair a Cropped Turtleneck with Ripped Sweatpants

Publish date: 2024-07-22

Jennifer Lopez's latest off-duty look brings a surprising denim trend to the world of athleisure.

The superstar was spotted heading to an LA dance studio in an elevated spin on her signature workout look. The "Jenny From the Block" singer looked cozy in a cropped knit sweater, featuring a loose turtleneck, and a pair of white drawstring joggers with distressed patches and two knee-baring holes. She also added a pair of black high-waisted bike shorts, which peeked over the waist of the sweats.

She wore her hair in a messy bun and finished the look with black tennis shoes and oversized gray aviators. She brought a hint of glam to the casual look with a pair of diamond stud earrings, and her go-to gym bag: a black Hermès Birkin.

Earlier this week, the Shotgun Wedding actor opted for a brighter monochromatic gym look, composed of a sky blue mock turtleneck sweater by A.L.C., matching leggings printed with an abstract painted pattern and the phrase, "My voice is heard," and chunky white sneakers.

She finished the all-blue look with a pair of tinted aviator sunglasses from Otra Eyewear with black rims, and carried her trusty Birkin.

Bellocqimages/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

While Rihanna will be the star of tomorrow's highly-anticipated Super Bowl Halftime Show, J.Lo will also be part of a can't-miss moment during the game's broadcast. People confirmed earlier this week that the multi-hyphenate will have a cameo in Dunkin' Donuts' first-ever Super Bowl commercial, which will star her husband Ben Affleck. Turns out the Air actor's surprise appearance at a Massachusetts location last month was part of the big ad, which will be the latest installment of the actor's ardent love affair with the doughnut franchise.
