Eminem is selling his Michigan mansion for 1.5m take a look inside

Publish date: 2024-09-26

Eminem has listed his home in Michigan for sale for $2million (around £1.55m). The Lose Yourself star looks set to lose money on the property, as he originally bought it for over twice the price – at $4.75million (£3.69million), 14 years ago – according to property website Trulia.

RELATED: See more celebrity homes here

The mansion spans 17,000 square feet and is set within a six acre estate in the suburb of Rochester Hills, Michigan. It has some pretty impressive facilities on offer too; along with an outdoor swimming pool and spa, there is also a guest house, tennis courts, entertainment cabana and private pond.

Within the house there are six bedrooms and nine-and-a-half bathrooms, along with a large kitchen and living room area.
