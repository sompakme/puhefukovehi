Tory Burch Women's Classic Rain Loafers

Oprah named Tory Burch’s rain loafers one of her favorites things in 2023, and I honestly can’t think of a better time to grab them. For starters, they’re half off right now, but it’s also the time of year when loafers are really in and water-proof shoes are a must. This style is available in three colors — white, black, and a spring-ready yellow — and, according to Tory Burch customers, are “truly divine” and made for “girls who want to look cute in the rain, too.” One person noted that the style is “durable” and “super comfortable,” while someone else described the feeling as “like walking on clouds.” “Plus, they're stylish and complement any outfit effortlessly,” they added.

Dock and Bay Reusable Makeup Pads

For just $10, you can grab these Oprah-approved reusable makeup pads. I know we all love our makeup wipes, but the reality is that they’re not great for the environment and are unnecessarily expensive when they could be a one-time purchase. These pads are “soft, functional…[and] gentle on skin,” according to shoppers, even removing “mascara and lipstick.” “I am amazed at the effectiveness of the cleaning. My mascara, concealer and anything else wipes off easily without rubbing,” raved one fan. And while these are machine-washable (they even come with a wash bag so you don’t lose them), customers noted that when you’re short on time, you can just give them a quick wash in the sink with face soap and let them air dry.

Bossy Cosmetics Luxe Lipstick Set

Bossy Cosmetics’ single lipsticks cost $20, but when you grab the set of four Oprah included in her 2021 gift-guide, you can apply an on-site 50-percent off coupon that brings it down to just $8 apiece. This set includes four matte colors — nude, berry, cherry red, and deep cranberry — that are formulated with moisturizing vitamin E. “It goes on like butter [and] stays on,” said one fan who deemed this the “best lipstick ever.” And others shared their sentiment, with someone else writing that it’s “long-lasting [and] hydrating” without looking “cakey.” “It stays on for hours,” they raved.
