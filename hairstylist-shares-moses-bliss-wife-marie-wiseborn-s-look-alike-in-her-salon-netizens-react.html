Hairstylist Shares Moses Bliss' Wife Marie Wiseborn's Look-Alike in Her Salon, Netizens React

Publish date: 2024-08-18
  • A hairstylist showed one of her clients, who she claimed looked like Marie Wiseborn, the wife of gospel singer Moses Bliss
  • In a video, she showed the hairstyle the client wanted, noting that it was what Marie was known for
  • The lady's natural hair was long and carefully styled and had the attention of several netizens who tried to see the resemblance

A hairstylist got the attention of social media users after she showed off one of her clients, saying she resembled Marie Wiseborn, the wife of Nigerian gospel singer Moses Bliss.

The client of the stylist (@foluflaunt_naturalhairsalon on Instagram) requested that her natural hair be styled in the same pattern Marie usually does hers.

From twisting her hair in bits, she weaved some parts in front and packed the back in a bun. The hair was beautiful, giving the client a Marie Wiseborn look in terms of rocking natural hairstyles.
