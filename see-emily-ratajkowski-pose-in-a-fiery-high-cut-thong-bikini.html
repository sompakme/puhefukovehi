See Emily Ratajkowski Pose in a Fiery High-Cut Thong Bikini

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Emily Ratajkowski is serving up plenty of sultry swimwear photos for the final weeks of summer.

Today, the model shared a new carousel of snaps taken in her sunlit kitchen, in which she shows off a printed string bikini from her Inamorata brand. The red-and-blue two-piece includes a triangle halter top and a thong bottom that rises high on the hips.

In some of the photos, the My Body author holds up her warm red hair with tendrils falling around her face. She went sans makeup and accessorized the look with a turquoise pendant necklace with an extra-long string, which she wrapped multiple times around her neck.

The multicolored set is the second bikini Ratajkowski has highlighted this week. On Wednesday, the model shared photos in which she lies on a carpet wearing a white ruched set comprised of an adjustable thong bottom and a cupped bra top with thick crisscross straps at the back. Her minimalist beauty look included straight hair, minimal makeup, natural nails, and a glossy lip.

The High Low With EmRata podcast host has been spotted showcasing her casually chic street style throughout the summer months, often while walking her dog Colombo. Earlier this week, she brought back a popular ’90s silhouette with her slinky, ab-baring look: a long black skirt from Silk Laundry and a twisted white crop top with billowing sleeves by House of CB. She added a pair of light brown leather boots, a gold chain belt, a slouchy black bag, and her go-to elongated black sunglasses.
