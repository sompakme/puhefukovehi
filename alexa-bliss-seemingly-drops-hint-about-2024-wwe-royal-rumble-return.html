Alexa Bliss Seemingly Drops Hint About 2024 WWE Royal Rumble Return

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Alexa Bliss has worked hard to become one of the most dependable and headline-worthy female Superstars in WWE. She is currently away from WWE television after her pregnancy and subsequent childbirth, and she now dropped a hint about returning at the Royal Rumble next year.

Alexa Bliss and Ryan Cabrera announced that they would have baby daughter. Alexa Bliss and her husband Ryan Cabrera then revealed that their daughter had been named Hendrix Rouge Cabrera.

After many months of anticipation, Alexa Bliss finally gave birth to her daughter last month. Her daughter’s birth also had very fascinating coincidence behind it for good measure. Obviously, fans were very happy for her in a big way.

Naturally, now fans have been wondering when she will return to in-ring action. Bliss took to her Instagram story and uploaded a photo of herself at the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event, seemingly hinting at returning at next year’s event.

Alexa Bliss also plans on preparing for her in-ring return very soon after giving birth, so it wouldn’t be impossible for her to return at the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event next year. Therefore, it remains to be seen what Bliss will do in the end.
