Robert William ‘Robbie’ Maddison is an Australian stunt rider who was born on July 14, 198He is from Kiama, New South Wales, and is known as ‘Maddo.’ Maddison was born in Caringbah and raised in Kiama Downs, where he honed his riding skills by participating in national motocross and supercross competitions. He graduated from Kiama High School in Year 10 and began an electrical apprenticeship. He proceeded to study freestyle techniques (FMX), and after mastering the abilities, he joined his first amateur tournament at Bachus Marsh, Victoria, and won both the amateur and pro events. He earned Gold in the X Games in Australia’s Wonderland in early 2004 after completing 13 back flips.

This raised notice of Australia’s new world beater.
