Khlo Kardashian fuels romance rumours with NBA player James Harden

Publish date: 2024-07-18

    Could Khloé Kardashian have netted herself a new man? The reality TV star has ignited rumours that she is dating NBA basketball player James Harden after they were spotted enjoying dinner together on Saturday night.

    The pair were seen dining together at Red 8, an Asian restaurant in Las Vegas. A source told that Khloé and James looked close and were "snuggling side by side" during their meal.

    The rumoured couple then continued their Fourth of July celebrations at Drai's nightclub, where Chris Brown was performing.

    Khloe Kardashian has been linked to NBA player James Harden

    It is not known how long Khloé and James have been dating but it is believed they may have met at Kanye West's birthday celebrations in June. Kim Kardashian hired out Staples Center for her husband and a group of friends to play basketball along with their favourite NBA players – including James who was one of the coaches.

    James is the first person Khloé has been linked to since she split from French Montana in 2014. The 31-year-old dated the rapper following the breakdown of her marriage to Lamar Odom.

    Khloé and Lamar are still legally married - she filed for divorce in December 2013 but the separation has yet to be formalised. Despite their imminent divorce, Khloé previously admitted she would always love her former husband.

    Khloe is still legally married to Lamar Odom

    "I will never ever not love Lamar," she confessed to . "I was so obsessed with him in a healthy way. He was my husband, and I still wish he was, and I don't think there's anything wrong with that."

    She added that although she and her ex are not really in touch anymore, she wishes things could be different.

    "There's nothing I can do. Lamar has to do things in order to change in that aspect, but yeah, if I had my way I'd be married to Lamar in two seconds."
