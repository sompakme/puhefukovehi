Man jokes about swapping SIM trays as a way of showing commitment

Publish date: 2024-09-11

- An American YouTuber recently went viral on social media for posting a hilarious way of showing his love

- Casey Neistat jokingly said he and his wife swap their SIM card trays on their phones instead of wearing wedding rings

- His tweet was met with an influx of jokes, memes, and puns from hundreds of tweeps

Everyone has their own definition of "showing love", whether it be grand gestures or expensive gifts.

One man jokingly shared his love language to Twitter and many tweeps thought he was being serious.

Casey Neistat, an American YouTuber shared pictures of he and his partner's phones.

He swapped the SIM card trays on both phones, which was obvious due to the colours of each cellphone.

Casey joked that he and his wife do not wear wedding rings, and show commitment to one another by swapping the SIM card trays on their phones.
