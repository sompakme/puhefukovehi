Anna Sui is an American fashion designer who was born on August 4, 196Sui is one of the most well-known names in American fashion, renowned for her timeless designs and ability to create collections that transcend periods via historical and cultural inspiration. In 2009, she received the Geoffrey Beene Lifetime Achievement Award from the Council of Fashion Designers of America (CFDA), joining the ranks of Yves Saint Laurent, Giorgio Armani, Ralph Lauren, and Diane von Furstenberg as one of the “Top 5 Fashion Icons of the Decade.” Clothing, shoes, cosmetics, eyeglasses, and accessories, as well as her famous range of trademark perfumes, are all part of her global luxury fashion business. Anna Sui’s goods are distributed in over 50 countries via her own shops and wholesalers. Sui’s fashion business was valued at over $400 million by Fortune magazine in 2006.
