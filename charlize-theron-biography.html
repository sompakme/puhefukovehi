Charlize Theron - Biography

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Charlize is an Oscar-winning actress

    Charlize Theron's early life

    Born on August 7, 1975, in the town of Benoni, Charlize Theron originally spoke Afrikaans and grew up on her parents' farm in Benoni, near Johannesburg. 

    Her father, Charles, was an alcoholic and was physically abusive towards her mother, Gerda. One night, her father, belligerently drunk and armed with a gun, fired three shots at his wife and child. Miraculously, neither Charlize nor her mother were hit by the bullets, but in self-defense, Gerda got hold of a handgun and shot and killed him. Charges were never brought against her mother.

    Charlize Theron's Hollywood career 

    Charlize initially began her career as a model. After treading the catwalks of Milan and Paris, she found herself in New York, where she enrolled in the renowned Joffrey Ballet School. Her dreams of becoming a prima ballerina were cut short, however, by a knee injury.
