How Tall Is The Tallest Building In Indianapolis

Publish date: 2024-08-22
People are currently reading this guide.

How High Can You Fly? Not Quite as High as the Top of This Skyscraper in Indianapolis

Hey there, fellow adventurer (or maybe you're just curious about really tall buildings). Today, we're ditching the hot air balloons and taking a virtual elevator ride to the top of the reigning champion of the Indianapolis skyline! But before we whoosh up to the observation deck with the wind whipping through our metaphorical hair, let's get down to brass tacks (or should we say rivets?).

Unveiling the Tallest Titan: The Salesforce Tower

Drumroll please... cue dramatic music The title of Indianapolis's tallest building goes to the Salesforce Tower, formerly known as the Bank One Tower and Chase Tower (buildings seem to go through name changes more often than we change our phone cases these days). This steel and glass giant boasts a height of a whopping 701 feet (214 meters), excluding those fancy antenna things on top. That's pretty darn tall, folks!

Think about it this way: if you stacked 233 average-sized giraffes on top of each other (because, why not?), you'd still be a little short of the Salesforce Tower's peak. Now that's a neck workout!

So, What Can You See From Up There? (Besides Your Dignity Floating Away)

Well, with that kind of height, you can practically see forever... well, almost! On a clear day, you can expect panoramic views of the entire Indianapolis cityscape, including that cool monument circle thingy (it's actually called Monument Circle, but where's the fun in that?). You might even be able to spot the nearest cornfield, because let's face it, Indiana.

But here's the real question: how much courage would it take for you to step out onto that observation deck and peer down at the tiny cars zooming by like ants? Just sayin', some of us might prefer to keep our feet firmly on the ground (or maybe 20 floors below the ground, at a nice cozy restaurant).

Fun Facts That Will Make You Sound Smart at Parties (or at Least Less Ignorant)

  • The Salesforce Tower was completed in 1990, making it a seasoned citizen of the Indianapolis skyline.
  • It holds the title of the tallest building in the entire state of Indiana, so basically, it's the king (or queen) of the castle.
  • Those pointy things at the top? They're not just for show! They actually help transmit radio and TV signals. So, the next time you hear your favorite song on the radio, you can thank the Salesforce Tower (or at least pretend to).

Burning Questions Answered: Your "How To" Guide to the Salesforce Tower

How to get to the top of the Salesforce Tower?

Unfortunately, the Salesforce Tower isn't open to the public for casual sightseeing. But hey, there's always the chance they'll need a taste tester for their office cafeteria (hey, a girl can dream!).

How can I experience the view from a tall building in Indianapolis?

There are a few other skyscrapers in Indianapolis with observation decks that are open to the public. Do some research and find one that tickles your fancy!

How tall am I compared to the Salesforce Tower?

Unless you're secretly a basketball player, you're probably much shorter. But hey, height isn't everything! You might have amazing skills or a killer sense of humor that the Salesforce Tower totally lacks.

How many floors does the Salesforce Tower have?

The Salesforce Tower is a proud owner of 49 floors. That's a lot of stairs (thank goodness for elevators!).

How much does it cost to build a skyscraper?

Let's just say it's a lot more than a lemonade stand would bring in. Skyscrapers are expensive business!
