Hon Abike Dabiri-Erewa, Governor Amosun become grandparents

Publish date: 2024-07-16

- Hon Abike Dabiri and Governor Ibikunle Amosun have become grandparents

- The in-laws welcomed their first grandchild recently

- Abike's son, Oladipupo married Amosun's daughter, Ayomide, on July 8, 2017

Seems like just yesterday, Oladipupo, son of senior special assistant to President Muhammadu Buhari on foreign affairs and diaspora, Hon Abike Dabiri, and Ayomide, daughter of outgoing Ogun state governor, Ibikunle Amsoun, tied the knot. Just a little over a year after their lavish wedding which held on July 8, 2017, the couple are now proud parents.

Reports reaching Legit.ng reveal that the couple welcomed their first child recently. Abike was there with the couple to welcome the baby. A picture of the latest grandma in town holding her first grandchild has emerged on the internet and people are sending congratulatory messages.

Many still remember how Ogun state was shut down in July 2017 for the nuptial of the new parents. Legit.ng had earlier shared photos from the ceremony that was packed with the creme dela cream of Nigeria.
