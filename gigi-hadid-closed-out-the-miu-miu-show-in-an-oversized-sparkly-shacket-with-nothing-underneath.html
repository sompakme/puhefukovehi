Gigi Hadid Closed Out the Miu Miu Show in an Oversized Sparkly Shacket With Nothing Underneath

Publish date: 2024-09-27

It's fashion week, which is basically our Super Bowl, and Gigi Hadid has been a star player in Paris. Gigi is to the runways what Travis Kelce is to the football field (it's called Swiftie math). So, while Gigi's bestie TSwift and their other friends were watching Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs dominate the turf on Sunday, the super model had about an excuse that was pretty much as good as it gets in terms of missing a friend's football festivities.

On Tuesday, Gigi walked in not one but two major fashion houses' Spring Summer 2024 shows, all in one day. Gigi stole the show at Miu Miu in a sparkly brown shacket and a matching pleated skirt that hit right below her knees. While she let her electric blue briefs peek out of her skirt waistband (a recurring motif in the brand's repertoire), she opted to wear nothing under her unbuttoned jacket. She accessorized with square specs, and her blonde hair was styled in a messy wet-looking updo.

Gigi closed out the finale alongside Priscilla star Cailee Spaeny, who wore an oversized brown trench coat, which she held closed, covering her minidress.

Earlier in the day, the famous model also appeared in Chanel's show at the Grand Palais Ephémèr, during which she wore a very shimmery jacket and trouser set accessorized with a chain belt, a necklace, and earrings all in geometric shapes.

Her blonde hair was worn down in crimped waves and a middle part and her minimalistic glam included bronzy cheeks and a pink lip.

Before jetting off to Paris to receive fashion month's MVP award, Hadid walked in Versace's mod show in Milan. She wore a pale yellow-green dress with a swooping cowl neck. Her blonde hair was worn in a high half ponytail held in place by a bow-shaped accessory.
