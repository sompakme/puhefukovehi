E-40 &amp; Draymond Green Star In New Beats By Dre Commercial

Publish date: 2024-08-07

Beats By Dre just released a new commercial specifically for the Bay Area, and it’s a trip down memory lane for anyone from the west coast 8-10 years ago. In the ad, Golden State Warriors Forward Draymond Green looks out over the Golden Gate bridge before putting on the seminal Hyphy hit “Tell Me When To Go” featuring Keak Da Sneak.

Draymond is digitally placed into scenes form the song’s official video, transported to a sideshow while wearing a shirt that simply reads, “THE BAY.” Other Warriors players make appearances in the video, as well as one Seattle SuperSonic and Oakland native: legendary point guard Gary Payton. This one will definitely do well out in Oakland.
