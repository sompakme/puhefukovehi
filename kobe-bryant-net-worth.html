He was also among the most paid athletes even after his retirement. He was 6ft 5in and a citizen of the United States of America.

Early Life

As mentioned earlier, Kobe Bryant was born in Pennslyvania in 1978. Joe Bryant, Kobe’s father, was one of the players of the 76ers and retired from NBA when Kobe was 6 years old. The family moved to Italy, and Joe, Kobe’s father, continued to play in the European league. Kobe Bryant would come to the US during the summer holidays to participate in basketball leagues. After Joe Bryant retired from sports, the family returned to Philadelphia, where Kobe went to Lower Merion High School and focused on his high school career.

During his life in high school, the American professional basketball player led a basketball team that, in turn, made it to the state championship for the first time in 53 years. He learned a lot about the game, contributing to his success in his playing career. The NBA legend was named the basketballer of the year at Gatorade’s Men’s National Basketball. He was the youngest player in NBA history.

Interesting Facts about Kobe
