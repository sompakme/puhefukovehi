Former WWE Superstar James Ellsworth Getting Championship Opportunity

Publish date: 2024-07-20

James Ellsworth had a nice run in WWE, but everything must come to an end. Just because Ellsworth is no longer on television every week doesn’t mean he gave up pro wrestling. If anything, he’s been busier than ever, and now he’s up for a title.

James Ellsworth’s time on the indies continues, and he kept working for GTS Wrestling and Grim’s Toy Show as well. Now, it seems that it is up for a title belt.

GTS Wrestling announced that James Ellsworth will be coming to wrestle in a championship match. YouTube has a championship, and now James Ellsworth could hold the title soon enough.

JAMES ELLSWORTH is the new challenger for the YouTube championship and he goes after Jimmy controversy today on #GTSWrestling

We will have to see if James Ellsworth is able to pull down the YouTube Championship. Only time will tell if he adds even more clout to that already established viral promotion’s roster while he’s at it.

James Ellsworth could always stop by WWE again for a RAW reunion or any other reason. After all, Triple H is calling the shots now, and anything is possible right now.
