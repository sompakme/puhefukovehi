Dwight Schrute's beet farm closed down

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Rainn Wilson's Office spin-off The Farm has been axed before hitting the small screen. Photo / Supplied

Rainn Wilson's The Office spin-off has been shelved by bosses at US TV network NBC.

Earlier this year, the comedian shot a pilot episode of The Farm, which centred around his character Dwight Schrute's life away from the office running a beetroot farm and bed and breakfast.

However, Wilson took to his Twitter page to let fans know the project has not been given the greenlight.

"Farm Update: NBC has passed on moving forward with The Farm TV show," he tweeted.
