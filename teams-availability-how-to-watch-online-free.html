The 2024 NWSL Challenge Cup features the NWSL champions, the team that won it all last season, battling the NWSL Shield winners, the team with last season’s best regular season record.

The match has Midge Purce and the NJ/NY Gotham FC taking on Alex Morgan and the San Diego Wave FC, respectively, at Red Bull Arena in Harrison, New Jersey, on Friday, Mar. 15 with kick-off at 5 p.m. PT/8 p.m. ET. The match broadcasts live on Prime Video.

If you want to catch all of the soccer action, keep reading to find out how to watch the NWSL Challenge Cup with and without a cable subscription, including ways to watch for free.

How to Watch the 2024 NWSL Challenge Cup Without Cable

Since the NWSL Challenge Cup isn’t being aired on traditional broadcast TV or cable networks, the only way to watch the NJ/NY Gotham FC vs. San Diego Wave FC match is to livestream it on Amazon.

While you can watch the match online for free with a free trial, the rest of the NWSL season’s matches on Amazon is only available to Amazon Prime members. Sign up for a 30-day Prime membership free trial to test out the service and stream the 2024 NWSL Challenge Cup on Prime Video.

Amazon Prime includes access to Prime Video; as well as fast same-, next-, or two-day free shipping on most items sold from the online retailer; in-store discounts at Whole Foods Market, access to exclusive shopping events — like Prime Day and Black Friday — and much more.

How to Watch the 2024 NWSL Challenge Cup With Cable

The NWSL Challenge Cup match final broadcasts on Prime Video. This means it’s unavailable on traditional broadcast and cable TV. If you’re not an Amazon Prime member, you’re going to have to sign up for a 30-day free trial to watch the match live.
