Naomie Olindo is back on Southern Charm with a new look thanks to plastic surgery

Publish date: 2024-08-21

Charleston residents are back on the screen with the arrival of Southern Charm season 8, featuring familiar faces like Venita Aspen, Craig Conver, Austen Kroll, and Kathryn Dennis. Among the returning cast members is Naomie Olindo, whose appearance has intrigued fans. Let’s delve into Naomie’s journey on Southern Charm and explore the transformations she has undergone, including plastic surgery.

Naomie’s Decision: Nose Job

In 2018, Naomie Olindo made a significant choice by opting for a nose job. She candidly shared that this was a decision she had contemplated since her childhood. Following the procedure, Naomie expressed her happiness, wishing she had chosen it sooner. To provide transparency to her fans, she even took to Instagram Stories to showcase her before-and-after photos, embracing the change openly.

Notable Weight Loss

Since joining Southern Charm in 2015, Naomie’s appearance has evolved over the years, drawing comments and admiration from fans. In a 2021 interview on “The Skinny Confidential Him & Her,” Naomie revealed that she follows a pescatarian diet and practices intermittent fasting to manage her weight. She adopted the habit of starting her meals with a smoothie, gradually building a healthier eating routine. Her weight loss journey took nearly a year, but the noticeable change was sudden and well-received by her followers.

Exploring Cosmetic Enhancements

In addition to her nose job in 2018, the 29-year-old reality star, Naomie, ventured into cosmetic enhancements in 202She sought the expertise of cosmetic injector Cameron Moskos for a rejuvenating transformation. The procedures included filler injections for her cheeks, chin, and jawline, along with some botox injections. Naomie was open about her aesthetic goals and shared the journey with her Instagram followers. The before-and-after photos of the cosmetic procedures showcased her refined features.

Embracing Change

Throughout her journey, Naomie Olindo has embraced change and personal growth. Her willingness to share her experiences, including plastic surgery and weight management, with her fans demonstrates her authenticity and transparency.
