CM Punk Emulates Randy Ortons Signature Pose After WWE Survivor Series

Publish date: 2024-07-27

WarGames took center stage as WWE brought the final premium live event of the year at Chicago’s Allstate Arena. Survivor Series featured the return of not just one but two megastars, as Randy Orton and CM Punk came back. In fact, Punk even emulated Orton’s signature pose after his return.

CM Punk shocked the entire pro wrestling world when he made his return at the very end of the Survivor Series Premium Live Event. Prior to this, Randy Orton also returned for the WarGames match – a moment that simply delighted fans.

As seen in footage Ringside News has obtained, CM Punk could be seen emulating Randy Orton’s signature pose on the ramp after returning. Interestingly enough, this was a direct response to Randy Orton reacting to Punk’s return as well, as Orton pulled up a chair and watched Punk’s entrance.

WWE is clearly at the very top of the world right now after bagging CM Punk again, following his AEW firing. It remains to be seen whether WWE will change for the better now that Punk is back.
