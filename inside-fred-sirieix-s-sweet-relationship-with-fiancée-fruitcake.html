Inside Fred Sirieix's sweet relationship with fiance Fruitcake

Publish date: 2024-09-16

The couple previously appeared on Celebrity Gogglebox together

    Fred Sirieix is a familiar face on our screens thanks to him being the face of the Channel 4 programme, First Dates. The Frenchman is adored for his romantic and charming personality when it comes to bringing potential matches together in the restaurant – but how much do you know about his own love life?

    The television star and maître d'hôtel is in a loving relationship with his partner and the couple are even engaged to be married. Want to know more? Find out about Fred's sweet relationship with his fiancée, Fruitcake, below…

    Who is Fred Sirieix's fiancée and what is her real name?

    Fred Sirieix is loved up with his fiancée Fruitcake and the couple went public with their relationship in 2018. Although Fruitcake is no stranger to the public eye having appeared alongside the star in , she has kept her real name and occupation private.

    When did Fred Sirieix and Fruitcake get engaged?

    Fred announced his engagement to his girlfriend of two years back in March 2020, but the pair held off their nuptials due to the pandemic. Speaking about getting down on one knee on GMB, Fred said: "It was perfect because she said yes. That's the main thing. The only thing though is I did not get a ring. But I've now managed to do it because we are out of lockdown, so the ring is there, and she is very happy. So all is well!"

    What has Fred Sirieix said about his wedding?

    During the same appearance on , Fred revealed that they were waiting for travel restrictions to ease so that they could celebrate with family and friends on their special day, which they are planning on taking place in Jamaica. 

    He said at the time: "Well we are waiting to see when we can travel as it's important to have friends and family there. We have to be patient and see what is going to happen – it is out of our control."

    Since then, there's no word yet on when they plan on tying the knot.

    Who else has Fred Sirieix been with?

    Prior to Fruitcake, Fred was in a relationship with Alex Spendonlini with whom he shares an 18-year-old daughter, Andrea Spendonlini-Sirieix, and a 14-year-old son named Lucien. Andrea is a professional diver for Team GB and recently won a Bronze medal in Berlin.
