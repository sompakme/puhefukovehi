Odumodublvck's Star-Studded Album Drops Dec 1

Publish date: 2024-09-19

Nigerian artist Odumodublvck is set to elevate the music scene by releasing the deluxe edition of his highly anticipated album, Eziokwu, scheduled to drop on December 1, 2023. The deluxe version promises to bring a new dimension to the original album, featuring an impressive lineup of guest artists.

The deluxe edition boasts seven new tracks, each showcasing a collaboration with some of the industry’s top talents. Among the notable guest features are Black Sherif, Nasty C, Mizzle, Duncan Mighty, Teni, BOJ, Masicka, Deco Suave, AGUNNA, and a joint effort with FatBoy E and Shagba.

Fans of Odumodublvck can anticipate an eclectic mix of musical styles and influences as each guest artist brings their unique flair to the tracks. With the addition of these collaborators, Eziokwu Deluxe is poised to be a standout project, offering listeners a fresh and diverse musical experience.

The announcement has generated considerable excitement among fans, who are eagerly anticipating the release of the deluxe edition. As the countdown to December 1st begins, music enthusiasts are gearing up for a musical journey that promises to leave a lasting impression on contemporary Nigerian music. Stay tuned for the much-anticipated drop of “Eziokwu” Deluxe, where Odumodublvck‘s artistry will undoubtedly shine alongside an array of exceptional talents.
