Alexa Bliss Says Goodbye To Her Abs After Pregnancy Announcement

Publish date: 2024-07-20

Alexa Bliss remains one of the most beloved WWE Superstars in the company right now as she worked hard to become a respected name in Vince McMahon’s company. Bliss has been absent from WWE television for a long time, but she announced that she is pregnant with her first child. Now it appears Bliss bid goodbye to her abs following her pregnancy announcement.

The former RAW Women’s Champion was last seen at the Royal Rumble Premium Live Event, where she squared off against Bianca Belair in a match for the RAW Women’s Championship. Bliss lost the match and she took time off right after that.

Since her absence, Alexa Bliss appeared on The Masked Singer, shot down a lot of trolls and also kept herself busy with numerous appearances on TV shows. Bliss also recently announced that she is pregnant with her first child, and family and friends rejoiced.

Little Miss Bliss took to her Instagram story and uploaded a photo of herself, showing off her baby bump. The former RAW Women’s Champion also bid goodbye to her abs for good measure.

“Goodbye abs. Hello little bump.”

Alexa Bliss also plans on preparing for her in-ring return very soon after giving birth. We will have to wait and see when Alexa Bliss will make her return to the squared circle, as fans simply want to see her back as soon as possible.
