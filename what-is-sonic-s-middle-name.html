Why is Sonic's middle name Maurice?

Trivia. Sonic telling Sally his middle name is Maurice, from Sonic the Hedgehog #0. Sonic's middle name was first revealed as Maurice in Sonic the Hedgehog #0, then again in Sonic the Hedgehog #53; his first name was not given in canon, though former head writer Ken Penders apparently intended it to be "Olgilvie".

Why is Sonic's name Ogilvie?

Sonic also has a true first name, but he shuts down discussion before it can be revealed in issue 53. A popular fan rumor has it that Penders used his personal website to reveal he intended it to be 'Ogilvie. ' This would make Sonic's true name Ogilvie Maurice the Hedgehog.
