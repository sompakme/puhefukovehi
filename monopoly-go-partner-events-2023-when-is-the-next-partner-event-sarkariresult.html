About Monopoly Go

Monopoly Go is an exhilarating new digital game inspired by the beloved Monopoly board game. Launched on April 11, 2023, it captivates players as they strive to amass wealth through strategic property acquisition, rental, and trading. The game has swiftly garnered substantial popularity since its release.

Partner Events in 2023

In 2023, Monopoly Go unveiled Partner Events, a resounding success with players. Events like the Thanksgiving Partners in Monopoly Go allowed friends to unite, relishing the game and reaping rewards. Each event had distinct start and end dates. Players could secure free dice rolls, cash rewards, and unexpected bonuses by accomplishing tasks and inviting friends. Participation in these events required baking gloves, attainable through landing on specific tiles or engaging in concurrent activities.

Participating in Partner Events

Engaging in Partner Events necessitates players advancing in the game, inviting friends, and fulfilling tasks to earn bonuses. Specific requirements and event-specific tokens might be prerequisites for participation in each Partner Event. Players can stay informed about upcoming Partner Events through in-game notifications or by visiting the official Monopoly Go websites.

The latest Partner Event was the Thanksgiving event, occurring between November 25 and November 26, 2023.
