Is there a referral program at Madison Reed?

Yes. When you refer a friend, you will receive a $15 Madison Reed code to use on your next order, while your friend will also receive $15 off their first order. It's a great way to make some excellent savings. 

Is there a Madison Reed membership program that I can join?

Yes. Choose one of the Limitless Hair Color Bar Membership programs to get $10 for every $300 you spend, 10% off all services and products, early access to new products, and loads more special perks. You may also receive an occasional Madison Reed hair color coupon. 

Are there any buy now, pay later offers at Madison Reed?

Absolutely. If you’d like to stock up on all your favorite hair colors, hair tools, and shampoo but would like to split your payment, Madison Reed will allow you to spread the cost using Afterpay. Customers will need to create an Afterpay account and returning customers can simply log in to their account to make their purchase.  

What is the Madison Reed returns policy?

If you'd like to return your Madison Reed order, you have up to 30 days from the date of delivery to send your item back. All returned items must be unused, unopened, and in their original packaging to receive a full refund or exchange.
