Bella Hadid Pairs Her Matrix-esque Trench Coat with an Unexpected Accessory

Publish date: 2024-09-28

Bella Hadid seems to be taking some fall fashion inspiration from The Matrix.

Yesterday, the supermodel attended Burberry's spring/summer 2023 aftershow party in London, where she flaunted an edgy all-black look.

Hadid kept warm in a long black leather trench coat layered over a crisp white collared shirt. She quite literally tied the look together with a shiny black tie, an unconventional accessory that may call English school uniforms or Men in Black to mind. For footwear, she opted for pointed knee-high leather boots in black. Other accessories included a forest green-framed pair of sunglasses, silver hoop earrings, a black square shoulder bag, and plenty of chunky rings.

Ricky Vigil M//Getty Images

Ricky Vigil M//Getty Images

The model's boyfriend, art gallerist Marc Kalman, accompanied her to the festivities in a color-coordinated outfit. He wore a black leather windbreaker, dark brown slouchy pants, and Doc Marten oxfords.

Ricky Vigil M//Getty Images

This month, the Kin Euphorics partner has been jetting across the globe to walk the runways for the Milan, London, and New York Fashion Weeks. Along the way, she has been spotted bringing her inimitable street style across the pond.

Hadid previously opened up about curating her iconic street style looks in an interview with WSJ. Magazine.

"I haven't had a stylist in a long time, maybe two years now," she said. "In the last year, it was really important for me to learn that even if people talk about my style or if they like it or if they don't, it doesn't matter, because it's my style."
