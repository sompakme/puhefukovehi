Henry Cavill, Kaley Cuoco reportedly call off romance

Publish date: 2024-10-02

Henry Cavill. Photo/AP

Henry Cavill and Kaley Cuoco have reportedly split after less than two weeks together.

The Man of Steel star and The Big Bang Theory beauty - who were spotted holding hands outside a Los Angeles supermarket earlier this month - have decided to go their separate ways after a few dates but are trying to remain friends, a source has confirmed to E!.

The news comes shortly after the 30-year-old hunk was said to be "totally hot for" the 27-year-old blonde actress, and the pair had been spotted out together in LA on several occasions.

A source explained: "They are totally hot for each other. He's excited to be dating her!"

Kaley also dropped a huge hint that the pair had started dating by posting a picture of herself looking at a poster for Man of Steel - which stars Henry as Superman - and adding the accompanying message:

"It's fantastic in every way."

Henry split from actress Gina Carano earlier this year following a 10-month relationship, while Kaley briefly dated Pepper bassist Bret Bollinger after ending her engagement to Josh Resnik in March 2012.

Kaley also previously dated her Big Bang Theory co-star and on/off on-screen boyfriend Johnny Galecki for two years, but the pair managed to keep their relationship a secret for the duration.
