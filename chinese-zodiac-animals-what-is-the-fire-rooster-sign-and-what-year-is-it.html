Chinese Zodiac animals: What is the Fire Rooster sign and what year is it?

Publish date: 2024-08-04

THE Fire Rooster in the Chinese zodiac represents those born in 1957 and 2017.

It is the 10th animal in the 12-year cycle and is also given to those born in 1933, 1945, 1957, 1969, 1981, 1993, 2005, and 2017.

What year is the Fire Rooster?

Chinese zodiac signs identify birth years with certain animals including the Rat, the Ox, the Tiger, the Rabbit, the Dragon, the Snake, the Horse, the Goat, the Monkey, the Rooster, the Dog and the Pig.

The Fire Rooster identifies those born in 1957 and 2017.

It is a sub-component of the Rooster in the Chinese zodiac. 

Each zodiac sign has its own element: Earth, Wood, Fire, Metal and Water, which add more uniqueness and characteristics to the sign.

Other years of the Rooster

The Rooster also represents those born in 1933, 1945, 1957, 1969, 1981, 1993, 2005, and 2017.

Roosters are said to be trustworthy and really good at managing financial affairs. 

Some notable Fire Roosters are Ellen DeGeneres, Steve Harvey, and Andrew Cuomo. 

Characteristics and personalities of the Fire Rooster

The Fire Rooster is very charismatic. 

They have a great business brain too and a terrific work ethic. 

They’re no softie though. Ruffle their feathers and await your execution, they take no prisoners.

Roosters are powerful and have an extraordinary presence. 

They know what they want and they certainly always get it. 

Everything this character does has a competitive edge - the Rooster regards pride as a primary trait, and they protect it and honor it with every ounce of their soul. 

Out of all the combinations in Chinese astrology, these charismatic creatures regard career and, ultimately, success, as the absolute number one goal in life.

Types of Roosters

Metal Rooster: Decisive, logical, popular. Need to work on communication and social skills.

Water Rooster: Proud but not boastful. Intuitive, supportive. They love beautiful things. These Roosters work well with others.

Wood Rooster: Jokesters, emotional, they crave affection. Rely on others too much, and need to learn to become independent.

Fire Rooster: Have strong time management skills and are trustworthy. Organized. They have rocky relationships but will settle in their middle ages.

Earth Rooster: Active, social, reliable, patient. Smooth career life, no efforts go to waste. The danger of becoming too proud.

Compatibility

The Rooster is most compatible with the Ox and the Snake. 

The Ox, Snake, and Roosters are considered deep thinkers. 

They are driven, goal driven and can be very persistent.

The Ox has stamina but can be dull but the Snake is highly diplomatic and sneaky in its way.

The Rooster is the head of the group and is always confident no matter the setback. 

However, the Rooster is least compatible with the Rat, Rabbit, Horse, and Dog. 

When partnered, these signs can be brutal with their opinions and criticism of each other.

The Rooster takes pride in being ambitious and a self-starter and can leave those jealous or anxious about Rooster’s nature.
