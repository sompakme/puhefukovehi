DJ Loonyo Wiki, Biography, Net Worth, Age, Career, Relationship,Family

Publish date: 2024-09-18
  • DJ Loonyo, also known as Rhemuel Lunio, is a talented artist from the Philippines.
  • He is a singer, dancer, professional DJ, choreographer, and social media personality.
  • Born on April 3, 1996, DJ Loonyo is currently signed under Universal Records.
  • On September 8, 2020, he released his debut digital single “Teka Muna.”
  • Originally from Cagayan de Oro, he now resides in Hangzhou province, China.
