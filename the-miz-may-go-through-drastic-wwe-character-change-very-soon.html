The Miz May Go Through Drastic WWE Character Change Very Soon

Publish date: 2024-08-18

The Miz is the A-Lister, and he’s known for drawing heat from fans as he continues listing his accolades on the screen outside the squared circle. The heat-seeking personality may be tamed soon enough.

Team #DIY are back together, and fans have waited a long time to see that happen. Tommaso Ciampa readily admitted that he wasn’t even sure about Team #DIY’s future in the company.

BWE noted behind their private X account that WWE is currently considering a big change for The Miz, as he could turn babyface to join Team #DIY. That would certainly be a big shift for the former MTV star.

“Few ideas discussed over a potential Miz face turn working with DIY. Not confirmed yet though.“

The Miz and Tommaso Ciampa have a history together, and things may not be settled just yet. Adding The Miz to Team #DIY’s already proven dynamic could also be very interesting to watch unfold.

Keep checking back with us here at Ringside News for more on this story, and so many more. The Miz has more stories to tell in the ring, and could have some fun with Team #DIY.
