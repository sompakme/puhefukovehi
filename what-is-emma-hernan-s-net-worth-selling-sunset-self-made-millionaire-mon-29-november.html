Meet Emma Hernan, Selling Sunset’s ‘self-made millionaire’

Season 4 of the Netflix show introduced Emma Hernan, a then 29-year-old CEO and realtor.

Hernan, 31, was born and raised in Scituate, Massachusetts with her family owning a seafood company.

Growing up as a teenager, she worked as a model and a babysitter before starting to invest in the stock market at age 14. She became Miss COED in 2015, a pageant to find “America’s college sweetheart”.

Emma now lives between Los Angeles and Boston, working as a real estate agent alongside her role as CEO of her vegan frozen food company, Emma Leigh & Co.

As a realtor associate for The Oppenheim Group, she is described on the website as a “self-made multi-millionaire”. She prides herself on being a “dynamic and relentless agent” for her clients in the city’s fast-paced real estate market.

What is Emma Hernan’s net worth?

There are numerous figures regarding her fortune, but most reports claim Emma has a $3 million net worth.

As a successful businesswoman, she reportedly helped finance her family’s business by age 17.

With a large social media following of more than two million users, Emma often shares insights into her working days via Instagram.

How long has she worked with The Oppenheim Group?

Emma Hernan first worked with Brett and Jason Oppenheim when searching for a house herself in 2017 and was encouraged to obtain a real estate license.

She later did so and joined The Oppenheim Group in 2018, and now refers to the founding twins as her big brothers.

Regarding her appearance as a realtor on Selling Sunset, Hernan stated that her entrepreneurial background gave her an advantage when it came to connections.

Emma continues to build her own real estate portfolio, purchasing residential and commercial investment properties in both Los Angeles and her native Boston.

Hernan also enjoys helping clients purchase investment properties with Oppenheim’s help, especially women looking to expand their portfolios.

If she’s not at the office closing deals, you’ll find Emma hanging out with her dog, Mr Benedict, aka Benny.
