NXT Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic Returning Next Week

Publish date: 2024-09-27

WWE started off 2024 with NXT New Year’s Evil, marking their initial special event of the year. The event showcased a significant start for the company’s third brand in 2024.

Ringside News provided complete coverage of WWE NXT’s New Year’s Evil show. It was quite a night, and there were a couple of notable headlines.

Ava has taken on a role of authority figure on the NXT brand. This week during New Year’s Evil, The Rock’s daughter reveled that next week’s episode of WWE NXT will se the return of the Dusty Rhodes Tag Team Classic.

Plenty of great teams have won that tournament, and cemented themselves into history. It will be interesting to see who wins the whole tournament this time around. It will all start next week.

We do not know who will be in those matches, but there is no shortage of hungry Superstars waiting for their chance to shine.
