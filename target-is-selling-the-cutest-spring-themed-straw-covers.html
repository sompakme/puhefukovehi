Instagram user TargetPursuit shared a video of the cute new straw-toppers, which are only available in stores, not online. Designs include a bunny rabbit, cactus, duckling, strawberry, flower, kitten, and more.

Why use straw covers? Well, if you drink out of a tumbler rather than a water bottle, then your straw is open to the environment. That means dust, germs, bugs, grime, and other mystery floaties can get into your straw and into your water. Straw covers help keep the water inside your Stanley cup or other tumbler fresh and hygienic.
