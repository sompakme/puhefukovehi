Leaked post-credit scenes from The Marvels confirm huge fan rumor (spoilers)

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Audiences enter the theater to see a Marvel movie with certain expectations in place. One of the most guaranteed is that everybody will remain seated until the house lights come up and the experience is over. Those who head for the door are guaranteed to miss out on an additional scene or two, and now that we’re in deep with Phase Five, extra sequences are sure to play a pivotal role in shaping the MCU’s future.

How many The Marvels after credits scenes?

Two credit sequences play in The Marvels, the first taking place mid-credits and the second and final stringer coming right at the very end of the Brie Larson blockbuster.

A handful of Marvel movies have opted to offer the fandom two credits sequences instead of one, The Marvels set on continuing the charitable tradition. Be sure to remain seated so you don’t miss out.

The movie opens exclusively in cinemas on Friday, November 10th 2020, and won’t be released same-day on Disney+.

The Marvels post-credit scene leaked

The first of two post-credit scenes has been leaked online, emerging on Twitter and Reddit threads confirming the return of Hailee Steinfeld as Kate Bishop, one of the central characters in the series Hawkeye.

She’s seen speaking to Ms. Marvel aka Kamala Khan, telling her “I want you on it.” Although what she’s referring to hasn’t been explicitly addressed in the leak, fans have suggested that she’s alluding to the Young Avengers, which will now likely include Kate, Kamala, Cassie Lang, and more.

The scene has garnered a lot of attention since it was leaked, and Kamala returning Kate’s offer with a beaming smile is as good as any verbal acceptance we could have gotten.

Next up, on the other hand, the second post-credit scene hasn’t been leaked in full but a screenshot has been shared around confirming the rumors… the X-Men are now part of the MCU, and worlds have collided at long last.

The screenshot features Maria Rambeau standing in front of the X-Men logo reminiscent of the Fox X-Men universe, teasing potential cameos from that original trilogy of movies starring Hugh Jackman as Wolverine and beyond down the line.

There have been rumors about the X-Men becoming part of the MCU in The Marvels for quite some time, with this post-credit scene leak finally confirming it to be true. Additionally, much has been made of the costume that Maria is wearing, with some fans claiming that she’s Binary, a duplicate of Carol Danvers originating from the comics.

The Marvels is in theaters Friday, November 10th 2023.
