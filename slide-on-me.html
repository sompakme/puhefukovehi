Slide On Me

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Frank Ocean continues to drop a steady stream of new music. After last month's "Biking" and the recent "Lens" series, Frank just premiered the Jeffery assisted cut "Slide On Me," which is a new version of an Endless track. The song is driven by a chilled out acoustic guitar, and neither Young Thug nor Frank Ocean are afraid of showing their vulnerability. With a vibe that's part rnb and part indie, "Slide On Me," is a perfect jam for those going through some romantic woes.
