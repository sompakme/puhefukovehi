16 Taylor Swift Lyrics That Work Even Better as Pick-Up Lines

Publish date: 2024-07-25

After announcing her much-anticipated 10th studio album, Midnights, at the 2022 VMAs, fans of the singer are finally getting ready to hear her new tracks that drop midnight on Oct. 21.

Related story 13 Pop Songs You Didn't Know Were Written About Real Celebrities

Unlike Swift’s previous two album releases, Folklore and Evermore, which were a total surprise for the fans, Swift has already revealed plenty about what’s to come. Swift shared that the songs are all inspired by “the stories of 13 sleepless nights scattered throughout my life,” Entertainment Weekly reported.

As for the track list, all the song titles have been revealed. Song titles include “Lavender Haze,” “Maroon,” “Anti-Hero” and “Snow on the Beach,” featuring none other than Lana Del Rey.

“This is a collection of music written in the middle of the night, a journey through terrors and sweet dreams,” Swift wrote on social media. “The floors we pace and the demons we face. For all of us who have tossed and turned and decided to keep the lanterns lit and go searching — hoping that just maybe, when the clock strikes twelve… we’ll meet ourselves.”

We’re excited for new music, of course, but partly because we’re running out of pick-up lines to use in the clubs because Swift is ideal for getting oh-so romantic. Try some of these out… if you dare.

Originally posted March 2015. Updated October 2022.

  • Tired of waiting

    Of course, you have to courageously approach a person with this one. Hopefully they’ll appreciate the irony.

  • You don’t know me

    Confidence or arrogance? Your targeted date will have to be the judge.

  • Your eyes

    Use it sparingly and on a target who either looks like they have a sense of humor or is seeking a soul mate.  

  • Trouble

    Bad boys… the world is full of them. Hand this pick-up line out like it’s candy.

  • Take me somewhere

    Cheese factor level: midnight. However, when delivered correctly, this line has a fairly high success rate.

  • Flawless

    Flattery gets you everywhere. 

  • Next to you

    While the emotional value of this line can’t be denied, be careful or you’ll come off as a stage-five clinger.

  • Love story

    Who would want to say no to a fairy-tale true love story?

  • Look at that face

    Jonah Hill’s character in Superbad made it clear that some people don’t have any problem being “that mistake.”

  • Won’t be sleeping

    60 percent of the time, it works every time.

  • Your smile

    Your target might think you’re a little high, but then again, who doesn’t like to hear that they have a nice smile?

  • For worse or better

    Be bold and commit and you can’t go wrong.

  • I know places

    The delivery of this line is key. You want them to feel like they’re being wooed, not being sized up by a serial killer.

  • Wildest dreams

    This whimsical line is for all the poets out there. It might be better used in a coffee house than a club.

  • Bad news

    This gruff line is a last-caller. It’s more of a demand than a request. 

  • Blank space

    How can you go wrong?
