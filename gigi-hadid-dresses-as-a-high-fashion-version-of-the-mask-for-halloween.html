Gigi Hadid dresses as a high-fashion version of The Mask for Halloween

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Gigi Hadid just won Halloween 2019 by dressing as Jim Carrey in hit '90s film The Mask.

The model arrived at Kendall Jenner's Halloween party in New York paying tribute to the 1994 movie - but instead of opting for Cameron Diaz's slinky dresses, she (refreshingly) eschewed the "sexy" costume option in favour of its cartoonish lead. Hadid was born in 1995, a year after the film's release.

Naturally, the 24-year-old put a high-fashion spin on the costume; her cropped yellow suit was from Italian fashion label Iceberg. She paired it with the matching wide-brimmed hat, crisp white shirt, Prada loafers, dotty braces and tie, plus - of course - green contoured make-up.

Sunset Boulevard//Getty Images

The model shared her look on Instagram, captioning her video and pictures with famous quotes from the film, including, "P-A-R-T-Y? Because I gotta!" and, "Sssomebody stop me!".

She credited make-up artist Patrick Ta, stylist Mimi Cuttrell, and hairstylist Laura Polko for helping her create the look.

Archive Photos//Getty Images

Meanwhile, the night's host dressed as a forest fairy in a golden mini dress and wings.
