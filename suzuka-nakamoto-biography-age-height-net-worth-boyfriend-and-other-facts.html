Suzuka Nakamoto Biography, Age, Height, Net Worth, Boyfriend And Other Facts

Publish date: 2024-09-11

Japanese music followers may disagree on a number of things, including the greatest singer of all time. Nonetheless, there will be a big consensus that Suzuka Nakamoto is one of the most beloved. The singer is known as the lead singer of the Japanese Metal-Pop band, Babymetal.

Suzuka Nakamoto Biography, Age

Suzuka Nakamoto was born on December 20, 1997 in Hiroshima Prefecture, Japan. The youngest of 3 sisters, it’s also where she was raised as a very talented child.

With a professional Japanese father who is a singer and member of a high school rock band, it’s no wonder his daughters are talented singers. Besides Suzuka, her sister, Himeka Nakamoto, is also a singer and the two form the group Tween. Himeka was part of the Japanese female idol group Nogizaka46.

Suzuka Nakamoto attended the Actor’s School Hiroshima (ASH) from where she graduated with her sister, Himeka Nakamoto. Before becoming a singer, she was in commercials; it was when she was only 5 years old. Even before she turned 10, she was already signed to Amuse, Inc., earning her a No. 2 ranking. 2nd Star Kids Audition.

After signing, Suzuka was created to be part of a group called Karen Girl. One of the group’s highlights has been their theme songs for Zettai Karen Children. After the series ended, the group disbanded after just one year.

She then became a member of Japanese idol group Sakura Gakuin in 2010. The group released their debut album, Sakura Gakuin 2010 Nendo: Message in 2011. It was from this group that the Babymetal Suzuka would be known in the whole world emerged. The group was subdivided into clubs and sub-units. One of the clubs is the Heavy Music Club and that’s where Babymetal, a sub-unit came from. Babymetal was created with Suzuka Nakamoto as the leader and Yui Mizuno (Yuimetal) and Moa Kikuchi (Moametal) as the members.

Babymetal quickly became very big and popular with its mix of heavy metal and various other genres. Her debut album, BABYMETAL was very successful in Japan and many other countries, topping the US and UK metal charts on iTunes.

With the band, Suzuka has won numerous awards, including Best Metal Act at the 2015 MTV Video Music Awards Japan, Best Japan Act at the MTV Europe Music Awards, and won Rock Goddess of the Year 2016. by Su-Metal at the sixth Loudwire Annual Music Awards in 2017.
