Pink's husband and daughter star in Alice in Wonderland themed music video

Publish date: 2024-09-17

    Pink recruited her husband Carey Hart and daughter Willow to appear in the music video for her latest single The 36-year-old star created the single especially for Disney's

    The video begins with Carey casually actually his wife if she'd like a cup of tea before walking into the room saying: "are you just going to hang around all day?". The singer is then revealed to be showing off her impressive acrobatics skills by hanging from aerial silks before her daughter runs through the room, chasing a butterfly through a mirror. Pink then follows little Willow, and the pair arrive in Wonderland.

    Willow laughed as she starred in the video with her mum 

    Little Willow looks like she's having the time of her time as she stars in the video with her parents, and looks adorable with her hair in brightly coloured pigtails while wearing an adorable butterfly dress as she giggles during the Mad Hatter's tea party. Pink, on the other hand, plays multiple Wonderland characters, including singing chess pieces and the Mad Hatter before falling back to the earth where she is wearing a straitjacket.

    The video ends with Casey having his wife committed to an asylum, calling out "love you babe!" to Pink then signing paperwork, asking the doctor: "she's going to be okay right?", before shrugging.

    The music video mirrors the plot of as it was revealed in the trailer that Alice's parents had her committed in an asylum before she escapes through the looking glass. The film will follow Alice (Mia Wasikowska) as she returns to Wonderland to save the Mad Hatter (Johnny Depp).
