Lisa Ray’s Early Life and Career Beginnings

Lisa Ray was born to a Bengali father and a Polish mother. She started her career as a model at the age of 16 and soon became a well-known face in the Indian fashion industry. Her breakthrough came when she appeared in the music video for Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan’s “Afreen Afreen”.

Acting Career

Lisa made her acting debut in the Tamil film “Nethaji” in 1996. However, she gained recognition in Bollywood with the film “Kasoor” in 2001. Her performance in Deepa Mehta’s “Water” in 2005 was critically acclaimed and brought her international recognition.

Hollywood Ventures

After making a mark in Bollywood, Lisa ventured into Hollywood with films like “I Can’t Think Straight” and “The World Unseen”. Her performances were well-received, further establishing her as a versatile actress.

Television Career

Lisa also made her presence felt on television. She hosted the Canadian show “Top Chef Canada” and the travel show “Oh My Gold”. She also appeared in popular shows like “Blood Ties” and “Endgame”.

Philanthropy and Social Activism

After being diagnosed with multiple myeloma in 2009, Lisa became a vocal advocate for cancer awareness. She has been involved in various philanthropic activities and has used her fame to raise funds for cancer research.

Brand Endorsements
