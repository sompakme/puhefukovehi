Is Howie Mandel Jewish?

Publish date: 2024-09-16
Spread the love

Howie Mandel, known for his candid critiques on America’s Got Talent, is also a skilled comedian. Recently, he co-hosted the Roast of Antisemitism with Rachel Bloom, sparking curiosity about his own faith. Let’s delve into the comedian’s religion and heritage.

Howie’s Jewish Heritage

Yes, Howie Mandel identifies as Jewish. His ancestors originated from Romania and Poland. In his unique way, he embraces his unorthodox Jewish identity, a sentiment shared by his parents who also follow Judaism. Despite being expelled from high school for his mischievous pranks, Howie remains devoted to his faith, often sharing openly about his religion.

The Tapestry of Howie’s Family and Heritage

Howie’s parents, Evy and Al Mandel, hail from Polish roots. Remarkably, he is a distant relative of the renowned Israeli violinist, Itzhak Perlman. Today, Howie has built his own family alongside his wife, Terry Mandel, since 1980.

Their union has blessed them with three children—Alex, Riley, and Jackie—adding layers of joy to their lives. Proud of his Canadian nationality, Howie is also a doting grandfather to two young boys. During his formative years, he attended the Hebrew School at Beth David B’nai Israel Beth Am in Toronto.

His educational journey also led him through three other Toronto high schools, including William Lyon Mackenzie Collegiate Institute. Here, a prank involving impersonation and construction led to his expulsion, a tale that now adds flavor to his comedic repertoire.

Roasting Antisemitism: Howie Takes the Stage

Howie Mandel recently took center stage at the Roast of Antisemitism in Los Angeles, alongside co-host Rachel Bloom. His stand-up often intertwines with his Jewish heritage, a practice he continued at the Saban Theater.

The show’s essence was to combat antisemitism with humor, culminating in Howie’s rallying cry for Jewish pride. In his distinctive style, he reminded the audience of their enduring history, urging them not to doze off during this crucial hour.

The event featured notable personalities like Michael Rapaport, all uniting in a jovial roast against those harboring prejudice. The evening was punctuated with Yiddish expressions, light-hearted circumcision jests, and even playful jabs at figures like Kanye West and Donald Trump.

Howie Mandel, a luminary on America’s Got Talent, stands tall not only for his entertainment prowess but also for his unyielding embrace of his Jewish roots. With humor as his compass, he navigates life’s ups and downs, leaving audiences both entertained and enlightened.
